Why Encrypt Planet ?

   It helps protect private information, sensitive data, and can enhance the security of communication between client apps and servers.In essence, when your data is encrypted, even if an unauthorized person or entity gains access to it, they will not be able to read it.

Use SSL/TLS

   The confidentiality and integrity protections offered by cryptographic protocols such as SSL/TLS can protect communications from malicious eavesdropping and tampering.

Protect Data at Rest

   It can also be used to protect data at rest. Data on a removable disk or in a database can be encrypted to prevent disclosure of sensitive data should the physical media be lost or stolen.

Confidentiality

   Confidentiality requires information to be secured from unauthorized access. This is accomplished by encrypting a sent message using a cryptographic algorithm with a key that's only known by the sender and recipient.
